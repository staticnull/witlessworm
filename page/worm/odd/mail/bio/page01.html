Name: Pete Martin
Age: 21
Hometown: Wauzeka, Wisconsin
My Parish: St. Wenceslaus, Eastman, Wisconsin
Interests/Hobbies: Traveling, Basketball, Weight Lifting, Socializing
Why Bike For Jesus? I wish to show the youth that being in the seminary does not necessarily mean that you are "locked-in" to the priesthood. I want to show them that it is merely a time to discern whether or not that little voice in their head that questions the possibility of the priesthood is a real call or something else. By Biking For Jesus, I hope to shatter any myths about seminary life that hold the youth back from entering. Through our efforts and by our example, I hope this much will be accomplished.
